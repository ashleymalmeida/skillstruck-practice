Dolce and Gabbana also known by initials D&G, is an Italian luxury fashion house founded in 1985 by two Italian workers named Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana. Versace the luxury House immediately became a leader in fashion and culture following its founding in 1978 by Gianni Versace, and it continues to lead with purpose under the creative vision of Donatella Versace.
